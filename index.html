How generation works (guaranteed solvable):
1. A random walk is generated that covers at least 80% of the grid cells.
2. The cells visited by the walk are flipped to "on".
3. The player starts at the beginning of the walk.
4. The goal is to step on all "on" cells to turn them "off".
5. The solution walk is provided as a hint.
